SUMMARY

Castle Academy of Early Education’s website desparately needed a redesign. As a Project Showcase for Bloc, I redesign modernize Beverlycastleacademy.org with a responsive website and new logo. I also re-edited copy, added a new color palette, typography and reorganized the sitemap for better user flows.


THE CHALLENGE

Giving Castle Academy a reboot challenged and reinforced all the skills I learned in my UX/UI course. I developed sitemaps, new user flows, and used Marvel for prototyping.

UI DESIGN

THE LOGO

I designed Caste Academy’s logo to include a castle and clover icon, to represent the Irish community of Beverly, and the castle itself that the academy is held in. I used a fun color palette, that looked educational, but youthful.

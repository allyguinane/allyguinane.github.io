  • MOBILE APP

    Charitable mobile app

    Charitable is an app that allows fundraisers to crowdfund donations for charities and foundations. The app allows donors to search and contribute to specific causes. Charitable’s logo includes a simple ribbon in the title for users to automatically recognize its association with charitable causes.

  • RESPONSIVE WEBSITE & APP

    Treasurely responsive website

    Treasurely is a SaaS (Software as a Service) web application that allows people to collect information and store it in a single place. A user can create notes, store images, bookmark links to websites and collaborate with other users within the app.

About Me

I'm a UX/UI Designer, Art Director and Journalist.  
Overall, I'm a fun, creative woman who so happens to really loves polka dots.
  • PHOTOS BY KATIE RYAN PHOTOGRAPHY

  • "Work Hard and Be Nice to People"

    Designing is truly my passion. My mission is clean design that showcases content and enhances the user experience. For nearly a decade, I've honed my visual skills as a print and digital graphic designer. Now I want to focus on using UX/UI design to solve problems, find solutions, create efficiencies, and reduce user frustrations. I want to design products that are helpful, easy to use and fun!

    I am a Chicago-based art director and UX/UI designer with experience in print and digital publishing. I have worked for a health-based magazine company for the past two years, creating design solutions to help clients understand their health plans and lead healthier lives.

    To further my career, I recently completed a 36-week UX/UI Design Course from Bloc.io. As a graduate, I am excited to start a new chapter in my career.

    I excel at using Adobe Creative Cloud, Sketch and Content Management Systems to create multi-platform content that melds style and substance. My graphic design skills are my strongest asset. However, my years of magazine and newspaper experience add an editor's careful gaze to my artist's eye.

    I have been fortunate to use my creative skills in publications and media – and turn it into a passion. The sky's the limit for this next adventure in UX/UI.
